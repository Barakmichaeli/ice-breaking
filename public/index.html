About Ice breaker

Nowadays meeting your future partner is very likely to happen through the online world. Application like Tinder, Facebook are very common and useful platforms for chatting with potential lovers. Even though, all those application are base on the very "old" fashion which is a simple and predictable conversations. This is where "Ice breaker" comes and offer an interactive solution for the initial interaction. We created a platform (demonstrated by a website) that creates "rooms" for two people only and gives them the opportunity to ask the other side questions about themselves with some funny and imaginary answers, from which the other side should guess.
